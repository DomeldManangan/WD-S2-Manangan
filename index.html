Headers and Footers

The header of an HTML webpage is its top part, similar to the title or logo at the beginning of a letter or newspaper. The footer, which is located at the bottom and functions as a signature or closing remark, contains information about copyright or contact data, much like the tiny print at the conclusion of a webpage. They both contribute to a webpage's organization and essential information provision.
